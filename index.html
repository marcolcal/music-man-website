It's Just Me

I am creating this website as a way to share my life story with my loved ones, both present and future. It's important to me that everyone knows who I am, where I came from, and how I came to be a born-again believer in Jesus. I was fortunate enough to grow up during a time of peace and prosperity, a time of freedom and adventure. My childhood was filled with joy and faith, thanks to my family who took me to church and prayed for me during my rebellious years.

I am truly grateful for my heritage and for those who prayed for me. Looking back, I can see how God protected me during my foolish and invincible years. Today, I pray for my children and grandchildren, that they too would follow after God and believe in the Lord Jesus Christ. He has completely transformed my life, and I know He can do the same for you.

I've been battling cancer for nearly 20 years, but by the grace of God, the battle is finally moving in my favor. I want my family and everyone who hears my music and reads my stories to know a few important things. We are all born in sin and need help to recover from it. The Bible says we are all condemned, regardless of how good or bad we may have been. But Jesus came to earth, was crucified and buried, to pay for our sins. He is the only one who can deliver us from a life of sin and death, and we must believe in Him to be saved.

I pray that everyone who reads my words, listens to my music, and reads my book, will discover the love of God and His plan for their life. True peace and direction can only be found in Him. Forgiveness and a new, full, and wonderful life are waiting for you if you give yourself to Jesus. Please, I urge you to do so!
